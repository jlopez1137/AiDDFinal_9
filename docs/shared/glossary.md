# Glossary

- **AI-First Development**: Practice of pairing human expertise with AI tooling while logging prompts and decisions for accountability.
- **Booking Conflict**: Time window overlap that blocks approval of a pending reservation.
- **Resource Owner**: Staff or authorized user responsible for publishing and moderating a resource entry.
- **Thread**: Messaging channel between requester and owner linked to a booking or resource.
- **Validation Log**: Evidence of tests, reviews, and controls verifying system behavior.
